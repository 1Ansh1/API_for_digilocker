Core Capabilities

OIDC Identity Assertions

Uses OpenID Connect (OIDC) ID tokens and signature checks to cryptographically verify claims with DigiLocker public JWKS keys.

Privacy Preservation

Zero persistent storage of cleartext PII (Names, DOB, Genders). Generates one-way cryptographic SHA-256 hashes for identity matching.

Append-Only Audit Trails

Tracks the session state transitions chronologically with immutable audit logging and system correlation IDs.
